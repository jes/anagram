Anagram Deputy

An extension of your brain that helps you find anagrams.

Anagram Deputy helps you find anagrams. It is not an anagram solver in its own right. There are lots of fully automated anagram solvers available if that is what you're looking for. Anagram Deputy typically allows you to find better anagrams, by augmenting your language processing abilities with your computer's search and keeping-track-of-lots-of-letters abilities.

In the top box, enter the phrase you're trying to find an anagram for. The letters available appear in blue, in a scrambled order. Start working on the anagram in the second box. The blue letters show you which letters are still remaining, and the small words underneath show you options for completing the word you're typing (or a new word, if none). Greyed-out suggestions are words that can be made, but with which a full anagram can not be made using the words in the dictionary. These are sometimes still useful in case you can use words that are not in the dictionary (e.g. names).

You can hit Ctrl to rescramble the blue letters.
