Anagram Deputy

a James Stanley project

An extension of your brain that helps you find anagrams.

The blue letters show you which letters are still remaining, and the small words underneath show you options for words.

You can hit Ctrl to rescramble the blue letters.

Red letters are letters you've used that aren't available.

I'd love to hear any feedback you have! Get in touch at jes@jes.xxx.
